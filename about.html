Our Story

Vero Financial was founded in 2021 by Sarah Martinez and David Park, two former Stripe engineers who saw firsthand how difficult it was for platforms to embed financial services into their products.

After watching countless companies struggle with fragmented banking relationships, compliance complexity, and slow integration timelines, they set out to build a unified infrastructure layer that would let any platform launch financial products in weeks, not years.

Today, Vero powers embedded finance for over 180 platforms, processing more than $4 billion annually. We're backed by leading investors including Sequoia Capital, Index Ventures, and Ribbit Capital.

What we believe

Infrastructure as a moat

Great infrastructure should be invisible. Our job is to handle the complexity so our customers can focus on building great products.

Security is non-negotiable

We handle sensitive financial data for millions of end users. Security isn't a feature—it's the foundation everything else is built on.

Move fast, carefully

In fintech, speed and caution aren't opposites. The best teams find ways to ship quickly while maintaining rigorous standards.
